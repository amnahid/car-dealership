================================================================================
  CAR DEALERSHIP & RENTAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
  Software Requirements — Vibe Coding Reference
  Broken into 3 Development Phases
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SYSTEM OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A comprehensive web-based platform to manage the entire lifecycle of vehicle
inventory — covering car purchasing, repair tracking, stock management, vehicle
documentation, sales (cash, installment, rental), customer management,
automated notifications, and financial/employee management.

Tech Stack Suggestions:
  - Frontend : React.js (or Next.js)
  - Backend  : Node.js + Express (or Laravel/Django)
  - Database : PostgreSQL or MySQL
  - Storage  : Cloud (AWS S3 / Cloudinary) for images & documents
  - SMS/Email: Twilio (SMS), SendGrid (Email)
  - Auth     : JWT-based role access control


================================================================================
  PHASE 1 — CORE FOUNDATION
  (Inventory, Purchases, Repairs, Documentation, User Auth)
================================================================================

GOAL: Build the backbone of the system — get cars in, track their condition,
manage documents, and control who can access what.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. USER AUTH & ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Roles:
  - Admin         : Full access to all modules
  - Manager       : Inventory, Sales, Rental management
  - Accounts Officer : Financial management, Salary management
  - Sales Agent   : Customer management, Sales records

Features:
  - Secure login system (email + password)
  - JWT session management
  - Role-based route/module protection
  - Activity logs (who did what, when)
  - Encrypted sensitive data storage

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. CAR PURCHASE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Record every car entering the system from a supplier.

Features:
  - Add new car purchase entry
  - Attach supplier details
  - Record purchase price and purchase date
  - Upload images of car condition at time of purchase
  - Upload purchase documents (PDF/images)

Data Fields:
  - Car ID (auto-generated)
  - Supplier name
  - Supplier contact details
  - Purchase price
  - Purchase date
  - Car details:
      - Brand
      - Model
      - Year
      - Engine number
      - Chassis number
  - Condition images (multiple uploads)
  - Notes / remarks

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. REPAIR & MAINTENANCE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Track all repairs done after purchasing a car.

Features:
  - Add repair records per car
  - Add individual repair costs
  - Upload before/after repair images
  - Auto-calculate total repair cost per car

Data Fields:
  - Car ID (linked)
  - Repair description
  - Parts replaced
  - Labor cost
  - Repair cost
  - Repair date
  - After-repair images

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. INVENTORY / STOCK MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Maintain a live, searchable list of all cars and their current status.

Features:
  - Real-time car inventory list
  - Car availability status tracking
  - Upload car photos
  - Search and filter cars (by brand, model, year, status, etc.)

Car Status Types:
  - In Stock
  - Under Repair
  - Reserved
  - Sold
  - Rented

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. VEHICLE DOCUMENTATION MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Track legal documents for each vehicle with expiry alerts.

Document Types:
  - Insurance
  - Road Permit
  - Registration Card

Features:
  - Add issue date and expiry date per document
  - Upload document files
  - Automatic alerts before expiration

Alert Rules:
  - Notify at 30 days before expiry
  - Notify at 15 days before expiry
  - Notify at 7 days before expiry
  - Alert channels: Dashboard notification, Email, SMS


================================================================================
  PHASE 2 — SALES, RENTALS & CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT
  (Cash Sales, Installments, Rentals, Customer Profiles, Messaging)
================================================================================

GOAL: Enable the business to sell and rent cars, manage customer records,
and automate all communications.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. CAR SALES MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  6A. CASH PURCHASE
  -----------------
  Features:
    - Record full cash payment sale
    - Apply discount option
    - Add agent commission
    - Generate sales invoice

  Data Fields:
    - Car ID (linked)
    - Sale price
    - Cash discount amount
    - Agent name
    - Agent commission amount
    - Sale date

  ---

  6B. INSTALLMENT SALE (Kisti)
  ----------------------------
  Features:
    - Create installment plan for a car
    - Auto-calculate monthly installment amount
    - Generate full installment schedule (month-by-month)
    - Apply late payment fine
    - Send payment reminders automatically

  Rules:
    - Minimum down payment required (configurable %)
    - Car delivery condition tied to payment percentage
      (e.g., car released after 20% or 30% paid)
    - Late payment triggers automatic fine

  Example Calculation:
    Car price      = 1,200,000 BDT
    Period         = 24 months
    Monthly amount = auto-calculated based on price + any interest/fee

  Notifications (auto-trigger):
    - Installment due reminder (before due date)
    - Installment received confirmation
    - Late payment notice (after due date passes)

  ---

  6C. RENTAL SYSTEM
  -----------------
  Features:
    - Create rental contract per customer
    - Customer identity verification
    - Set rental duration (start/end date)
    - Track rental cost
    - Rental income logging

  Customer Profile Data (for rentals):
    - Full name
    - Phone number
    - Address
    - National ID number
    - Driving license number
    - Photo upload
    - Emergency contact

  Rental Notifications:
    - Rental confirmation message (on contract creation)
    - Rental expiry reminder (before end date)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Central hub for all customer records.

Features:
  - Create and manage customer accounts
  - View full customer history
  - Track all purchases and rentals per customer
  - Document storage per customer

Customer Record Contains:
  - Purchase history
  - Rental history
  - Installment records
  - Outstanding payments / dues

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. AUTOMATED MESSAGING SYSTEM
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Auto-send relevant messages to customers at key events.

Message Types & Triggers:
  - Sales      : Thank-you message after purchase
  - Installment: Payment reminder (before due date)
  - Installment: Payment confirmation (after payment received)
  - Installment: Late payment notice (after due date)
  - Rental     : Rental confirmation (on contract creation)
  - Rental     : Return reminder (before rental end date)

Delivery Channels:
  - SMS (via gateway, e.g., Twilio or local BD SMS API)
  - Email (via SendGrid or SMTP)
  - WhatsApp (optional — via WhatsApp Business API)


================================================================================
  PHASE 3 — FINANCIALS, HR, DASHBOARD & REPORTING
  (Accounting, Employee Management, Reports, Admin Dashboard)
================================================================================

GOAL: Give management full financial visibility, HR tools, and a power-packed
dashboard with exportable reports.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. ACCOUNTING & FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Track all money in and out, calculate profit per car and overall.

Expense Tracking:
  - Car purchase cost
  - Repair/maintenance cost
  - Employee salaries
  - Other operational expenses

Income Tracking:
  - Cash car sales
  - Installment payments (per payment received)
  - Rental income

Reports Generated:
  - Profit per car (income minus purchase + repair costs)
  - Monthly profit/loss summary
  - Sales report (date range filterable)
  - Expense report (date range filterable)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10. EMPLOYEE & SALARY MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: Manage staff records and salary payments.

Features:
  - Create/edit employee profiles
  - Define salary structure per employee
  - Record salary payments
  - Track bonuses and commissions

Data Fields:
  - Employee name
  - Role / designation
  - Base salary amount
  - Payment history (date, amount)
  - Bonus / commission records

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
11. DASHBOARD & REPORTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Purpose: One-screen overview of the entire business status.

Dashboard KPIs (Live):
  - Total cars currently in stock
  - Total cars sold (this month / all time)
  - Total cars currently rented
  - Pending installments (overdue + upcoming)
  - Upcoming document expiry alerts
  - Total revenue (this month / all time)
  - Total profit (this month / all time)

Reports (Exportable — PDF/Excel):
  - Car inventory report
  - Sales report
  - Rental report
  - Installment report (with overdue flags)
  - Profit and loss report

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
12. SECURITY & DATA MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Secure login with hashed passwords
  - Role-based access control enforced on all routes
  - Automated database backup system
  - Encrypted storage for sensitive fields (NID, license, financials)
  - Full activity log (user, action, timestamp) for audit trail

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OPTIONAL FUTURE EXTENSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Mobile app (React Native / Flutter)
  - SMS gateway integration (local BD provider or Twilio)
  - WhatsApp Business API integration
  - Cloud storage for all images and documents (AWS S3 / Cloudinary)
